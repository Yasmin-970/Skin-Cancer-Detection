Transfer Learning

We utilized EfficientNet-B0, a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network, leveraging Transfer Learning. The final classifier head was modified to include Dropout layers and Batch Normalization to prevent overfitting and ensure robust performance on skin lesion images. Data augmentation techniques like random rotation, flipping, and color jitter were applied to enhance generalization.
